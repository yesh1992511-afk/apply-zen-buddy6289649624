JobPilot Capture — Settings

Paste the pairing token from /extension in your JobPilot dashboard. The token authorizes this browser to send the jobs you view into your account.

Cookie sync (optional, advanced)

If enabled, your logged-in session cookies for the supported job sites are encrypted with your passphrase here in the browser and uploaded to JobPilot. The VPS worker uses the same passphrase to decrypt and reuse the session, so auto-apply skips the login wall.

Security: the passphrase NEVER leaves this browser. The server only stores ciphertext. Use the same passphrase in the VPS worker's .env as COOKIE_PIPE_PASSPHRASE. If you lose it, just re-enter it here and on the VPS — cookies will be re-uploaded on the next 12-hour cycle.
